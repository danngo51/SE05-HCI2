Bibimbap

Estimated time to cook: 45 min
Servings:

Ingredients

For the Bibimbap Bowl:

  • Rice: 2 cups cooked short-grain rice (Korean or sushi rice)
  • Vegetables:
    • 1 carrot (julienned)
    • 1 zucchini (julienned)
    • 100g spinach
    • 100g bean sprouts
    • 5-6 shiitake mushrooms (sliced)
  • Proteins (Optional):
    • 200g beef (sirloin or ground beef)
    • OR substitute with tofu, mushrooms, or fried egg

Instructions

  1. Cook the Rice: Rinse the rice several times until the water runs clear. Cook in a rice cooker or on the stovetop according to package instructions. Keep warm.
  2. Prepare the Vegetables:
    • Spinach: Blanch in boiling water for 30 seconds, drain, and season with salt and sesame oil.
    • Bean Sprouts: Blanch for 2-3 minutes, drain, and season similarly to spinach.
    • Carrot & Zucchini: Stir-fry with oil and salt for 1-2 minutes each.
    • Shiitake Mushrooms: Stir-fry with sesame oil until soft.
  3. Cook the Beef (or Alternative Protein): Stir-fry beef or tofu with garlic and soy sauce.
